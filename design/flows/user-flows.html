FinClaude — User flows

Navigation & decision flows derived from the specification use cases.

1 · Authentication & first-run onboarding

From first app open to a fully set-up account ready to take its first snapshot. The gate middleware on the backend enforces setup completion before any other endpoint is usable.

2 · Snapshot gap detection & entry

After login, the backend computes missing snapshot dates from the account's start date + periodicity. The user fills them oldest-first; there is no skip.

3 · Asset & goal management (main app loop)

Once past the gate, the user works inside three primary tabs. Core CRUD flows are intentionally symmetrical to keep the app shape predictable.

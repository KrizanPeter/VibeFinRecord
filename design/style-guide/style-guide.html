Design System

Visual foundation for a modern personal-finance tracker. Clean, calm, trustworthy — designed so users can confidently see their whole wealth at a glance.

Design principles

The rules we design by — every decision should reinforce one of these.

Clarity over decoration

Financial data must be legible at a glance. Generous whitespace, strong typography, no visual noise.

Trust through restraint

Restrained color palette and careful motion. The app should feel like a calm bank, not a trading app.

Long-term perspective

Design for the person planning decades, not minutes. Emphasize trends, totals and direction of travel.

Low-friction data entry

Snapshots are the core habit. Every tap toward completing one matters — forms are big, fast and forgiving.

Brand color

A grounded green — signaling growth and stability without the jittery energy of pure fintech neon.

FinClaude Brand

A grounded, honest brand for people who want to see the full shape of their wealth — without trading-app noise.

Logo — primary mark

A stylised F composed of stacked horizontal bars — shorter ones at the bottom, the longest on top — reading as both "F" and as a rising chart. The offset dot represents today's snapshot, sitting on the growth line.

Wordmark lockup

Mark + wordmark paired at fixed proportions. "Fin" stays ink-900; "Claude" picks up the brand green to emphasise the app's identity.

Clear space

Always reserve clear space around the mark equal to half the height of the "F". Nothing should intrude.

Voice & tone

The brand's verbal identity complements the visual one.

We are
  • Calm & precise
  • Long-term focused
  • Clear about tradeoffs
  • Honest about uncertainty
We are not
  • Hypey or gamified
  • Loud with emoji
  • Trading/crypto bro
  • Paternalistic

Don'ts

A few rules that keep the brand recognizable.

Don't recolor the mark outside the brand palette.

Don't skew, rotate, or distort the mark.

Don't place the mark on a low-contrast colored background.

Don't drop the mark below 60% opacity.
